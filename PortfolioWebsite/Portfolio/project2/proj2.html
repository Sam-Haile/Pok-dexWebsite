What can I do?

The player comes equipped with a sword that can strike in all directions, making sure any threat can be taken care of. In addition, the player is able to counter enemy attacks. If timed correctly, the enemy will recieve damage and be launched back. Stuck in a tight spot? Try jumping off walls or climbing them to evade your pursuers and find secrets hidden in the caves.

Chromatic

  • Tales of Trove is a personal passion project that I have been working on since the beginning of September 2022. It was made in using the Unity framework with C#.

  • The game is an adventure platformer similar to WarioLand and Spelunky, in that it puts emphasis on exploration and collecting various items and treasure.

  • Players will traverse various different terrain, obstacles, and enemies while attempting to find all four gems before time runs out. Once found, venture deeper into the cave in search of the most priceless item. The CROWN.

  • Throughout the process of creating this game, I gained a lot of experience while working in the Unity engine that can be utilized in future projects wether on Unity or another systems
